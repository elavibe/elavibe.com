WuTang SuperGrover

My friend Jaiye asked me to make him a unique piece of art. So I took it as a challenge to really make something different. The WuTang SuperGrover is two of Jaiyes favorite things, so I meshed them togehter. This is built with Plywood, and painted with airbrush/paintbrush. Grover is mounted with magnents so the WuTang logo isn't ruined.
